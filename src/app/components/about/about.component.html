About DE-CHAT

DE-CHAT is a completely decentralized web-application built using Solid data interchange technology in order to give users complete control over the data that they send.

Solid is a technology invented by Tim Berners-Lee, the creator of the modern internet, that gives users complete control of how their data is exchanged. With DE-CHAT, data that you send is completely owned and controlled by you, with no central entity being able to view it during or after the sending process. In a traditional messaging application, data that you send typically passes through a central entity for processing, which makes your data vulnerable for others to interfere with. Without this central entity, your data is safe and maintained by you and you alone.
